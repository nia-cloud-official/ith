    Services

    We offer a comprehensive suite of services to ensure our students and industry partners receive the highest quality support and resources. Our services are designed to enhance learning, provide career advancement opportunities, and foster industry connections.

    Professional Training Programs

    We provide a range of diploma and certification programs in tourism and hospitality management, culinary arts, hotel operations, event management, and more. Our courses combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare students for real-world challenges.

    Workshops and Short Courses

    Our specialized workshops and short courses focus on current trends and emerging skills within the industry. From sustainable tourism practices to digital marketing in hospitality, we cover diverse topics that help students and professionals stay ahead in the industry.

    Internship and Job Placement Services

    We work with top-tier hotels, resorts, and tourism companies to offer valuable internship opportunities, giving students practical experience and a pathway to employment. Our dedicated placement team also assists graduates in finding full-time positions in their chosen fields.

    Career Counseling and Development

    Our career services team provides one-on-one counseling, resume workshops, interview preparation, and networking events. We help students and alumni build strong professional profiles and connect with industry leaders for lasting career growth.

    Alumni Networking and Mentorship

    Our alumni network provides graduates with ongoing support and opportunities for professional development. We organize mentorship programs and networking events to connect recent graduates with experienced professionals who can offer valuable guidance.

    Research and Industry Insights

    Our research center conducts studies on tourism trends, hospitality innovation, and market analysis to help students and partners stay informed of industry shifts. We publish reports and host seminars to share insights on sustainable practices, customer engagement, and emerging technologies.
